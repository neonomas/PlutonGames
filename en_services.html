Board game afternoons

Wednesday from 17:00 to 20:30

Join us to test fresh additions to our library, like the latest 'Catan' expansions, the madness of 'Exploding Kittens', or resource management games like 'Terraforming Mars'.

If you don't have anyone to play with, this is the perfect place to find a group. We have a store expert to explain the rules of open games so you don't have to read manuals. Come spend the afternoon!

Max capacity: 20 people. Reservation is recommended.
Warhammer 40k league

Thursday from 16:30 to 21:00

The fierce local Warhammer 40k league of this season continues. Bring your customized 1000-point army and show your skills as a strategist on our tables with professional scenery, recreating war-torn planets.

If you are new, you can come as a spectator or join a friendly learning match at the adjacent table with armies provided by the store!

Friday night Magic (Modern)

Friday at 17:00

The classic tournament that kicks off the weekend. This month, the format chosen by the community is Modern. Play best-of-three Swiss rounds and compete for local glory.

We raffle promotional packs (Promo Packs) from the latest set among all participants regardless of the outcome, and give store credit to the top 4 players. Sharpen your deck and join the largest MTG community in the area!

Masterclass: Miniature painting

Saturday, June 13, 11:00 to 14:00

Learn advanced techniques like dry brushing, washes, edge highlighting, and creating realistic bases with our guest professional painter, specialized in competitive armies.

The workshop price includes all the material you need to use during the class (fine brushes, wet palettes, full set of Citadel paints) and a basic miniature (Space Marine or Stormcast Eternal) to practice on and take home finished.

Thursday 18th events
Warhammer 40k league

From 16:30 to 21:00

Regular league matches at 1000 points. The standings are very tight and this week the playoff brackets will be decided.

Indie comic presentation

At 18:00

An intimate talk and signing session with the author of the acclaimed dark fantasy comic "Heroes of Cardboard". Discover their creative process firsthand, from the sketch to the final panel, and get an exclusive print with your signed copy.

Store championship Pokémon

Saturday, June 20, Starts at 10:30

The most important competitive event of the season at the store level! Prove that you are the best trainer in Vigo at our official Store Championship. Standard format, best-of-three Swiss rounds with a cut to Top 8.

The absolute champion will win an exclusive "Store Champion" playmat that cannot be obtained any other way and a full booster box from the latest expansion. Additionally, we will give out commemorative stamped promo cards to all attendees and raffle official merchandise between rounds.

Magic new set prerelease

Saturday, June 27, Starts at 16:30

The community's favorite event is back! The Sealed format where you open cards for the first time. Be one of the first to experience the magic of the new set before its global retail release.

We will hand you a sealed prerelease kit containing 6 booster packs and an exclusive date-stamped foil promo card. You'll have 45 minutes to build a 40-card deck and participate in a casual and friendly tournament, without the pressure of the competitive meta. Ideal for veterans and new players alike. There will be a pizza round mid-tournament included with your ticket!

Advanced Yu-Gi-Oh! tournament

Mondays at 17:30

It's time to duel. Start the week with our official Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG tournament in Advanced format. Prove the worth of your deck against the fiercest duelists in Vigo.

An OTS pack will be given to each player for participating, and we will distribute exclusive material such as playmats or Field Centers depending on the final attendance.

Casual Lorcana tournament

Tuesdays at 17:00

Enter the magical world of Disney Lorcana TCG. A friendly event aimed at both new players (Illumineers) and veterans. Casual constructed format.

Earn points for the bi-monthly league just for playing and win foil promo cards and exclusive Disney character pins at the end of the season.

MTG Pioneer tournament

Saturday, June 6 at 10:30

Take your Pioneer deck for a spin in our special weekend tournament. A more accessible format than Modern and perfect for competing at a high level.

Generous store credit prizes for the winners based on the total ticket revenue. Come claim your spot!

Intro to D&D workshop

Sunday, June 14, 10:30 to 14:00

Have you always wanted to play role-playing games but didn't know where to start? Join our table for a quick game (One-shot) designed for absolute beginners in Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition.

Our Dungeon Master will provide pre-generated characters, dice, and cheat sheets. You only need to bring your imagination. Extremely limited spots (only 5 adventurers).

Family sunday

Sunday, June 28, 10:30 to 14:00

A morning dedicated to the little ones and their families. We will open dozens of children's board games and light party games (like Ghost Blitz, Dobble, and Ticket to Ride First Journey).

Our store "elves" will be available to help you choose the perfect game and explain how to play so you can start laughing and playing right away.
