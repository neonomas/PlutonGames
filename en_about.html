Our identity

Discover who we are, how we started, and why we are so passionate about alternative leisure.

The player's haven

Plutón Games is much more than a local shop in Vigo; we are a living space dedicated to the analog leisure and culture sector.

With a meticulously selected catalog, we are proud to be a warm meeting point where fans can feel at home, try new releases, and forge new friendships.

What are we?

A digital and physical showcase for board games, trading cards, novels, and comics. We select the best from each world to offer you a vibrant and updated catalog.

Genuine community

Fleeing from cold online formats, our value lies in specialized advice. Open a box, try the games, and enjoy our vibrant tournament calendar.

Our mission

Born from a love for good narratives, we aim to be the epicenter of alternative leisure. We promote healthy, analog, and social entertainment in a fun environment.

Our crew

To ensure your experience (both physical and digital) is impeccable, our team is structured into three key areas working in perfect harmony.

Management and direction

Charting the roadmap. We handle the long-term strategic vision, ensure the supply of the best new releases, and maintain solid relationships with our publishers and distributors.

Sales and player service

The first smile you see when you enter. Our specialized team advises you at the counter, teaches you how to play the newest games, and answers your online queries with infinite patience.

Marketing and events

The masters of ceremonies. They keep our social networks alive, update the online catalog and, most importantly, orchestrate all the weekly face-to-face tournaments and gatherings.
